Features of Firecrackers

1. Bright Colors

Firecrackers come in various vibrant colors like red, blue, green, and gold, adding excitement to celebrations.

2. Loud Sounds

Different crackers produce various sound levels, from mild pops to loud booms that echo through the night.

3. Types

Sparklers, rockets, flower pots, and bombs are popular cracker types with different visual and sound effects.
